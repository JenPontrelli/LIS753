Friends of Palo Alto Library
Used Book Sale

Saturday, November 4 & Sunday, November 5, 2023
Thousands of gently used books and materials waiting to be discovered!

Come browse through our collection of over 50,000 gently used books, records, videos, magazines and more for sale at bargain prices starting at 50 cents.

Sale Locations and Prices

Main Room: Adult hardbound books starting at $1 and paperback books starting at 50 cents.

New Room: Children's books, music, videos and toys starting at 50 cents.

Rooms K6 & K7: Bargain Books! Sale items including all hardbound books for $1, paperbacks for 50 cents and children's books for 25 cents. On Sundays all bargain book items are half-off or fill a grocery bag for just $5.
