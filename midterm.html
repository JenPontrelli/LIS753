Thousands of gently used books and materials waiting to be discovered!

Come browse through our collection of over 50,000 gently used books, records, videos, magazines and more for sale at bargain prices starting at 50 cents. The sale is held the first full weekend of every month, 8am-6pm, throughout the library. Due to wide popularity, ticketing and item limits may be instituted. See our Admittance & Ticketing page for more details!

Sale Locations and Prices

Main Room: Adult hardbound books starting at $1 and paperback books starting at 50 cents.

New Room: Children's books, music, videos and toys starting at 50 cents.

Rooms K6 & K7: Bargain Books! Sale items including all hardbound books for $1, paperbacks for 50 cents and children's books for 25 cents. On Sundays ll bargain book items are half-off or fill a grocery bag for just $5.
